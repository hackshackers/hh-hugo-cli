  12. Still, that doesn't mean progress with the technology will come so fast. Making drones feasible will require advances in materials and batteries, said Tyler Brown, who is working on the Occucopter project. On the software side, drones aren't aware of their environment and can't fly well autonomously, which requires advanced sensors and learning algorithms. Because of the difficulty in flying accurately, drones are involved in frequent crashes and require constant repairs.
